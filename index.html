Presentation

Our association was created to help people in need. The literacy rate continues to increase in the world but 750 million people still cannot read or write correctly according to UNESCO, at the origin of the International Literacy Day on Saturday. That is why we decided to act to change the trend. We are conscious that it is going to take time but it is our duty to make things change. Each human has rights on Earth and living in good conditions without reading and counting is not possible.

Our goal

Our main goal is for you to find a job as a developer. To achieve this, we will teach you how to read and count. Once this is done, we will teach you how to code. This is a path you must follow to achieve our goal. To achieve this, you will have full-time face-to-face training. When you are ready with your new skills, we will look for an internship with you to make your achievements a reality. Be motivated because we are more than anything to help you achieve your dreams.
